Earthquake Scenario

You're in Your Classroom When...

The ground starts shaking! Learn the proper "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" technique to stay safe during an earthquake.

Safe Spot: Under Desk

Drop down and take cover under a sturdy desk. This protects you from falling objects.

Danger: Near Windows

Stay away from windows! Glass can shatter and cause serious injuries during earthquakes.

Exit Route

Remember the exit, but don't run during shaking. Wait until it stops, then evacuate calmly.
